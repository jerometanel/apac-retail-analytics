# Key Findings — APAC Retail Purchase Path Analysis

## Finding 1 — Entry Category Predicts Upgrade Path
New clients enter via Fragrance (24%) and Skincare (20%).
Clients who purchase Skincare as their second product 
upgrade to Regular at 55% — the highest conversion rate 
of any product combination.

**Action:** Recommend a Skincare product within 90 days 
of any first Fragrance purchase.

## Finding 2 — Handbag Purchase is the Elite Trigger
62% of Regular clients who purchase a Handbag upgrade 
to Elite within 6 months.

**Action:** Introduce entry Handbags (22 Bag, Boy Bag) 
to Regular clients with 3+ purchases and avg order > SGD 600.

## Finding 3 — China and Hong Kong Hold Highest Opportunity
235 New and Regular clients across China and Hong Kong 
represent SGD 192,000 in estimated upgrade revenue 
if moved to Elite segment.

**Action:** Prioritise outreach to CN and HK market 
client service teams for upgrade campaigns.
